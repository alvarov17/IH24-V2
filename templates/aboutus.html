About Us

Welcome to Medical Chain! Our purpose is to use a decentralized blockchain to our (and your) advantage for purposes of security and protection of sensitive material. By using our services, you may upload and protect your medical records at no cost than what it normally takes to secure and store medical records. Here at Medical chain we share a vision of a future where our personal records are protected at a much lower cost while also making it impossible to have information leaked as the blockchain cannot be edited or hacked into.
